Cinnamon Swirl Coffee Cake

(310)

This delicious dessert recipe contains a 9x13-inch cake that tastes like a cinnamon roll. Everyone will be begging to try a bite!

By:
Joe Smith
Published February 18, 2021
Prep: 15 mins Servings: 24
Cook: 30 mins Yield:One 9x13-inch cake
Total: 45 mins

Ingredients:

  • 3 Cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 Cup white sugar
  • 1 ½ Cups milk
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 4 Teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 Teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ¼ Teaspoon salt
  • ½ Cup unsalted butter, melted and cooled

Topping:

  • 1 Cup brown sugar
  • 2 Tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 Tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 Cup unsalted butter, cubed

Glaze:

  • 2 Cups powdered sugar
  • 5 Tablespoons milk
  • 1 Teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, milk, eggs, baking powder, vanilla, and salt for cake together in a large bowl until completely combined. Slowly pour in butter and mix to incorporate; pour into the prepared baking pan.
  3. Whisk brown sugar, flour, and cinnamon for topping together in a bowl. Cut in butter with 2 knives or a pastry blender until well combined. Drop evenly over the batter, then swirl with a knife.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 30 to 40 minutes.
  5. Mix powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla for glaze together in a small bowl. Drizzle over the warm cake.
