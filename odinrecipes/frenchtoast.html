Homestyle French Toast

(310)

Is there nothing better than French toast for Sunday breakfast? Thick slices of bread, soaked in a mixture of beaten eggs with milk and cinnamon, toasted in a frying pan, and served with butter and maple syrup. This has to be one of our favorite, and most indulgent, breakfast dishes. This French toast recipe is different because it uses flour. Read on below if you too would like to create this delicious breakfast dish.

By:
Jane Doe
Published September 5, 2021
Prep: 10 mins Servings: 12
Cook: 20 mins Yield: 12 slices
Total: 30 mins

Ingredients:

  • ¼ Cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 1 Pinch salt
  • 1 Eggs
  • ½ Teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 Teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 Tablespoon white sugar
  • 12 Thick slices of bread

Steps:

  1. Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in the milk. Whisk in the salt, eggs, cinnamon, vanilla extract and sugar until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Soak bread slices in mixture until saturated. Cook bread on each side until golden brown. Serve hot.
